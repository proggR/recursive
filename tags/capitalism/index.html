sourced.fm: Restructuring the Music Industry With Community Driven Social Capitalism

published on May 21, 2016
Video may have killed the radio star, but industrial money killed music. It wasn’t its first time doing it. My fear is that unless we rally behind something better, its not going to be the last either. The truth is the aristocracy has always found its ways to co-opt music for commoditized profits and influence. Throughout the renaissance era this came through the patronage model as patrons sought to influence culture by playing patron to the arts, primarily in pursuit of increased esteem among peers in a time when culture was associated with class.
