US Rejects Russia's Prom Date In Syria: Does Not Collaborate Or Coordinate With The Russians On Any Operations in Syria

published on May 22, 2016
After proposing joint force strikes in Syria on Friday, Russia has been promptly shut down by the US. Pentagon spokesman Navy Captain Jeff Davis made clear that the US does “not collaborate or coordinate with the Russians on any operations in Syria”, going on to point out Russia’s support of Assad. In the end, nothing changes. Syria’s still rekt and remains the backdrop of a proxy war. story: Yahoo News
