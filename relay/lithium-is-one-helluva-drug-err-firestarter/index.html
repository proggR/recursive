Lithium Is One Helluva Drug... Err... Fire Starter

In response to the missing EgyptAir plane, Capt John Cox explains that lithium batteries pose an increasing risk for airlines. Estimates show that aboard a plane of 100 passengers, there could be over 500 lithium batteries stowed away in the cabin. While unlikely, these lithium batteries could potentially cause a fire if they were to become punctured or otherwise damaged.

One of the millions of reasons we desperately need solid state battery tech.
