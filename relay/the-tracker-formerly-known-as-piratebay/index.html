The Tracker Formerly Known AS thepiratebay.org Is Once Again thepiratebay.org

The internet freedom trolls at the pirate bay have managed to close the loop in their border hopping fight to keep their tracker alive by once again returning to a temporary safe haven at thepiratebay.org, the domain their story started at.

To recap, this adventure started in 2012 when the Pirate Bay switched from .org to .se under threat of seizure by US law enforcement, which was followed by successive leaps to .gl, .is, .sx, .ac, .pe, .gy, and back to .se. Following that, the site shotgunned six domains at once, but after their .gs, .am, .gd, .mn, and .vg domains were under threat, they switched to the .se domain and added .ms. Now with the latter shut down and the former once again finding itself under threat, the Pirate Bay has returned to its .org roots.

For now.
